Portal Manager
Create, manage, and share client portals. No code needed.
📋 How it works
1. Create a portal — Click "+ New Portal", fill in the client name, property, and select a color.
2. Add documents — Paste Google Drive or OneDrive links for OMs, LOIs, and marketing materials.
3. Share the link — Click "Copy Link" and send it directly to your client.
4. Data is live — Activity, marketing stats, and prospects pull from Airtable automatically.
